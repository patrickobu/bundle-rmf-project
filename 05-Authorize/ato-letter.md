## Authorization Decision

I, Linda Reyes, Chief Information Officer and Authorizing Official
for the Bundle Hospital Network, hereby grant an

**AUTHORIZATION TO OPERATE**

to Bundle — Electronic Healthcare Management System (BUNDLE-EHMS-001).

This authorization permits Bundle to process, store, and transmit
Protected Health Information (PHI) and other sensitive data as
described in the System Security Plan (Version 1.0, 2025) in support
of the Bundle Hospital Network's clinical and administrative operations.

---

## Basis for Authorization

**(a)** Bundle's security controls are appropriate for a HIGH impact
system, implemented consistent with NIST SP 800-53 Rev. 5 HIGH
baseline as tailored for Bundle's healthcare and cloud environment.

**(b)** The independent assessment by ClearPath Security LLC confirms
that the core technical controls protecting PHI — encryption, access
control, network security, and monitoring — are robustly implemented.

**(c)** The six findings in the SAR represent manageable residual risks
that do not constitute an unacceptable threat to patient safety or PHI
confidentiality given the compensating controls in place and the
remediation commitments in the POA&M.

**(d)** Bundle's operational necessity to the hospital network's
clinical mission justifies accepting the residual risk under the
conditions stated below.

---

## Conditions of This Authorization

**CONDITION 1 — IR TRAINING (Due: 30 days)**
The ISSO must provide documented evidence confirming that all 500
Bundle staff have completed mandatory IR awareness training.
This is a HIGH priority condition.

**CONDITION 2 — POA&M REMEDIATION (Per individual deadlines)**
All six POA&M items must be resolved by their stated target dates.
Monthly POA&M status reports are due to the AO.

**CONDITION 3 — CONTINUOUS MONITORING (Ongoing)**
The ISSO must maintain the continuous monitoring programme as defined
in the RMF Step 6 documentation. Quarterly posture summaries are
due to the AO.

**CONDITION 4 — CHANGE NOTIFICATION (Within 5 business days)**
Any significant change to Bundle's architecture, data processing scope,
or operating environment must be reported to the AO.

**CONDITION 5 — BREACH NOTIFICATION (Immediate)**
Any confirmed or suspected PHI breach must be reported to the AO
immediately. The HIPAA breach notification process will be activated.

---

## Reauthorization

This authorization is valid for three years from the date of issue,
expiring in 2028. Reauthorization must be initiated no later than six
months before expiry.

Early reauthorization is triggered by:
- A confirmed PHI data breach
- A HIPAA enforcement action
- A major change to Bundle's architecture
- A significant change in the threat landscape
